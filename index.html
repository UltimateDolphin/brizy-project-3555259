Introduction

The .ShopNow domain is a call to action domain designed to drive visitors to your online shop.

Blockchain Based

Your domain will exist on the Handshake blockchain, a decentralized system with none of the restrictions imposed by the traditional ICANN domain system.

Claim Your Brand

Get first mover advantage on the blockchain web by claiming your dream domain on .ShopNow and simply redirecting it to your existing ICANN website.

For Engagement

.ShopNow isn't just a label, it's a call to action designed to encourage people to shop now. Call to actions are a marketing staple and a must have for user engagement.

Access Handshake Domains in 60 Seconds

Use Cases for .ShopNow

Here are some suggested use cases to help you get the most out of your .ShopNow domain name.

For Retailers

florist.shopnow, artsupplies.shopnow, pctech.shopnow...

You don't need to process payments online to keep an online presence for your store. Build a retail shop website and direct visitors to contact you via phone and email. List all your physical store locations on your website and keep visitors informed about your latest specials available in-store.

For Online Shops

booksonline.shopnow, fashiononline.shopnow, pcaccessories.shopnow...

Blockchain websites don't support SSL yet but you can still take payments using the usual options used by static websites such as taking orders by phone, email as well as platforms like Gumroad and Facebook.

Important Notes

Two important points to note when using handshake domains.

Don't Collect Sensitive Information

SSL is currently not supported by handshake so be mindful of what data you collect via the site if any. Directing users to 3rd party systems to make contact or to process data is recommended. Examples: Click to email, Discord, Telegram, Send a Facebook Message.

Email Is Available Through Hmail

Visit Hmail.app to setup private email on any handshake domain. The current cost is 2.5 hns per month for private email using any handshake domain name you own. Examples john@stationary.shopnow, mary@flowers.shopnow
